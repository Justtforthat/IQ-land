"Пятнашки" is a puzzle consisting of 15 square dice with numbers from 1 to 15. All dice are enclosed in a 4x4 square box (the side of the box is four times longer than that of the dice). Thus, when placing the knuckles in the box, one empty space is left the size of one knuckle, you can use it to move the knuckles inside the box.

The object of the game is to arrange the placement of the numbers in the box, placing them in ascending order from left to right and from top to bottom, starting with the number 1 in the upper left corner and ending with an empty space in the lower right corner of the box.
